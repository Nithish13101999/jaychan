Frequently Asked Questions

What data or proof do you have that these products are in high demand?

We share live sales data from the Flipkart and Amazon portals. Additionally, we provide live data from certified applications for each product we offer and operate with.

What are the product margins, and how much profit can I expect after marketplace fees and other operational costs?

We assure a 15-20% profit margin and around 9% net profit for fast selling, low-ticket products at an average selling price of ₹169 to ₹499. For high-ticket items, we assure an overall margin of 35-40%. The net profit depends on how you operate and sell.

Are these products competitive, and how will they stand out against similar products already on the market?

The truth is, every product faces competition, whether in terms of quality, price, or problem-solving ability. However, we achieve the best results by focusing regionally. Regional domination is key to standing out. Plus, our pricing is highly competitive in the Indian market, matching the best prices available. We also have a unique strategy regarding this competition that you may find intriguing.

Will you provide a clear breakdown of how fees will affect my margin?

Yes, we provide selling prices for all marketplaces and the estimated settlement amount for each product. We also give you a detailed marketing budget and offer access to a paid tool to help manage returns and other operational factors.

Will you help manage listings, pricing, and performance analytics, or is this a one-time service?

No doubt! We offer continuous support throughout your business journey. We also provide help with uncommon experiences via community gatherings.

Will you help with warehousing, shipping, and returns management?

We offer drop shipping to Amazon and Flipkart fulfillment facilities. However, if you run a local order processing unit, the responsibility lies with you. We do provide all the necessary packing materials along with the order. Additionally, we help you access detailed reports from the portal.

Will you provide selling materials like images and videos for the products we purchase?

Yes, we provide complete selling materials for both online and offline platforms. For online, we offer images, videos, and A+ content. For offline, we supply hard-copy catalogs, which are shipped regularly to ease the sales process.
